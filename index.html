About Me

My Story

Growing up in a rapidly changing urban environment sparked my passion for understanding how cities evolve and how we can shape them for the better. This curiosity led me to pursue urban planning, where I discovered the perfect intersection of creativity, analytical thinking, and social impact.

Throughout my career, I've been fortunate to work on projects that range from local community initiatives to international development programs. Each project has taught me something new about the complexity and beauty of urban systems.

When I'm not working on planning projects, you'll find me exploring cities around the world, photographing urban landscapes, or reading about the latest innovations in sustainable development. I believe that the best planners are also curious observers of human behavior and urban life.

"Cities have the capability of providing something for everybody, only because, and only when, they are created by everybody." - Jane Jacobs
